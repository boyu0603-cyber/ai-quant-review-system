# Fit with SUTD MSc in Design and Artificial Intelligence for Enterprise

## Design

AI Quant Review System begins with a design problem: how can high-uncertainty
candidates be reviewed in a structured, risk-aware, and auditable way?

The project frames this as a workflow design challenge involving candidate
normalization, risk gates, decision notes, post-event learning, and write-back.

## Artificial Intelligence

AI is used to assist candidate structuring, risk factor organization,
review-question drafting, and post-event reflection.

The project does not position AI as an autonomous trader or final
decision-maker. AI output remains a review aid.

## Enterprise

Although the public case uses market review as the domain, the underlying
workflow is relevant to enterprise review systems.

Organizations also need to structure candidate opportunities, apply risk gates,
record decisions, audit review quality, and learn from outcomes.

## Human-AI Interaction

The project emphasizes human-AI collaboration. AI helps structure the review,
while the human reviewer decides whether to watch, reject, or review later.

## Responsible AI

Responsible AI boundaries include:

- No financial advice
- No automated trading
- No real signals
- No proprietary strategy rules
- Human-in-the-loop review
- Explicit risk and uncertainty
- Public-safe documentation only

## Risk-Aware Decision Support

The project focuses on risk-aware decision support by making uncertainty,
missing information, and review quality visible before any follow-up decision.
